## Privacy Policy May 2026 This application (“Page Automator”) is a private tool used solely by the owner to manage and automate content on Meta related Pages owned by the administer. Information Collected This application does not collect, store, or share personal information from users. The app may access Meta Page data (such as posts, page metadata, and engagement metrics) strictly for the purpose of managing and publishing content on pages owned or administered by the app owner. Use of Data Any data accessed through Facebook APIs is used exclusively to: Create and manage posts Schedule content Retrieve page-related information for operational purposes No data is sold, shared, or used for advertising. Third-Party Services This application interacts with Meta’s Graph API to perform its functions. All data handling is subject to Meta’s own policies and terms. Data Security This application is used privately and is not publicly accessible. No user accounts or external user data are maintained. Changes to This Policy This policy may be updated as needed to reflect changes in functionality or requirements.
